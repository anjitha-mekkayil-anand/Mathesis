# AZ-204 Certification Enablement Guide (Synthetic)

> Synthetic demonstration document. Domain names and weights are simplified for demo
> purposes and do not reproduce the official exam blueprint.

## Who this certification is for
Cloud Engineers building and maintaining applications on Azure. Internal role mapping:
Cloud Engineer — primary certification AZ-204, secondary AZ-305.

## Skill domains and study emphasis
| Domain | Exam weight | Study emphasis |
|--------|------------|----------------|
| Develop Azure compute solutions | 30% | Functions, App Service, containers — build one small app per service |
| Develop for Azure storage | 20% | Blob + Cosmos DB access patterns, consistency levels |
| Implement Azure security | 20% | Managed identities, Key Vault, app configuration — most-failed domain internally |
| Monitor and troubleshoot solutions | 15% | Application Insights instrumentation, log queries |
| Connect to and consume services | 15% | API Management, Event Grid, Service Bus messaging patterns |

## Recommended preparation
- Total recommended study: ~20 focused hours over 3–4 weeks
- Hands-on labs matter more than reading: 60/40 lab-to-reading split recommended
- Internal learners who passed averaged 22+ study hours and 76%+ practice scores
- The security domain is the most common internal failure point — schedule it early,
  revisit it last before the exam

## Readiness signals before booking
- Practice score sustained at or above 75%
- All five domains self-rated 4+ after lab completion
- At least one end-to-end build combining compute + storage + managed identity
