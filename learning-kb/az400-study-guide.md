# AZ-400 Certification Enablement Guide (Synthetic)

> Synthetic demonstration document. Domain names and weights are simplified for demo
> purposes and do not reproduce the official exam blueprint.

## Who this certification is for
DevOps Engineers designing delivery pipelines and operational processes. Internal role
mapping: DevOps Engineer — primary certification AZ-400 (AZ-104 or AZ-204 assumed).

## Skill domains and study emphasis
| Domain | Exam weight | Study emphasis |
|--------|------------|----------------|
| Design and implement processes | 25% | Agile flow, traceability, release strategy design |
| Implement CI/CD pipelines | 30% | GitHub Actions + Azure Pipelines — build both at least once |
| Implement security and compliance | 20% | Secret management, dependency scanning, pipeline permissions |
| Design a monitoring strategy | 25% | Telemetry design, dashboards, alerting and SLO thinking |

## Recommended preparation
- Total recommended study: ~25 focused hours over 4–5 weeks
- This certification rewards production experience: pair every domain with a real or
  sandbox pipeline change
- Internal learners who passed averaged 26 study hours; those under 16 hours mostly
  did not pass
- Monitoring strategy questions are scenario-heavy — practice interpreting requirements,
  not memorising service names

## Readiness signals before booking
- Practice score sustained at or above 75%
- Built one complete CI/CD pipeline with gated release and rollback
- Can explain secret rotation and least-privilege pipeline identity without notes
