# DP-203 Certification Enablement Guide (Synthetic)

> Synthetic demonstration document. Domain names and weights are simplified for demo
> purposes and do not reproduce the official exam blueprint.

## Who this certification is for
Data Engineers designing and operating data platforms on Azure. Internal role mapping:
Data Engineer — primary certification DP-203.

## Skill domains and study emphasis
| Domain | Exam weight | Study emphasis |
|--------|------------|----------------|
| Design and implement data storage | 30% | Lake layout, partitioning, Synapse vs dedicated pools |
| Develop data processing | 35% | Spark + Data Factory pipelines — the heaviest domain, start here |
| Secure data solutions | 20% | Encryption at rest/in transit, RBAC, managed identities for data services |
| Monitor and optimize data solutions | 15% | Pipeline monitoring, query tuning, cost-aware design |

## Recommended preparation
- Total recommended study: ~22 focused hours over 4 weeks
- Data processing (35%) deserves at least a third of total study time
- Internal learners with heavy meeting load (20+ hrs/week) showed markedly lower
  completion on this track — protect focus blocks before starting
- Build one ingestion-to-serving pipeline end to end; most failed questions trace to
  never having operated a full pipeline

## Readiness signals before booking
- Practice score sustained at or above 75%
- One end-to-end pipeline built and monitored for a week
- Storage design domain self-rated 4+ — it anchors scenario questions across the exam
